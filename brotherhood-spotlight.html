Brotherhood Spotlight

Recognizing the brothers of Beta Phi who are making an impact — in their careers, communities, and the lives of those around them.

The legacy of Beta Phi isn't only found in national offices and historic publications. It lives in the doctors, educators, engineers, entrepreneurs, public servants, and community leaders who carry this brotherhood into every room they enter. This page is dedicated to spotlighting those brothers — past and present — whose stories deserve to be told.
