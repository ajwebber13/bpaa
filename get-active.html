Support current Beta Phi brothers and Dillard University by becoming an active member of the Beta Phi Alumni Association.

Membership Tiers

Choose Your Membership

All membership dues go directly toward supporting the Beta Phi Chapter, funding scholarships, and advancing the mission of the Beta Phi Alumni Association.

Golden Brothers

$85 / year

Members who have been in Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity for 50 or more years. A tier honoring the lifetime dedication of our most senior brothers.

Beta Phi Brothers

$138 / year

All members who do not fall within the Golden or Young Brother categories. This is the standard alumni membership tier for active BPAA participation.

Young Brothers

Contact Us

Members who have graduated from Dillard University within the last five years. A special tier supporting our newest alumni as they begin their post-graduation journey.
