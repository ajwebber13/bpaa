Membership

Get Active

Your brotherhood doesn't end at graduation. Join BPAA and keep the legacy of Beta Phi moving forward.

The Beta Phi Alumni Association welcomes all brothers initiated through the Beta Phi Chapter of Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity, Inc. at Dillard University. Whether you crossed decades ago or just completed your undergraduate journey, there is a place for you in BPAA.

Your membership directly supports current Beta Phi brothers on campus, contributes to the endowed scholarship at Dillard University, and sustains a brotherhood that has been active since 1938.

50+ Years of Brotherhood

Golden Brothers

Brothers who have been members of Alpha Phi Alpha for 50 or more years. Your legacy is the foundation of everything we do.

Lifetime of Service

Golden Brothers have given decades to this fraternity and this chapter. BPAA honors that commitment with lifetime recognition and a permanent place in our history.

Mentorship Role

Golden Brothers serve as mentors and advisors to younger brothers — passing down wisdom, history, and the standards that have made Beta Phi great.

Special Recognition

Golden Brothers are honored at all BPAA events and anniversaries, and their profiles are preserved in the chapter's permanent historical record.

Recent Alumni

Young Brothers

Brothers who have graduated within the last 15 years are the lifeblood of BPAA's future. Your energy, networks, and proximity to the current chapter experience make you vital to our mission.

Young Brothers are encouraged to get involved in mentorship programs, chapter fundraising, event planning, and service initiatives that support current Beta Phi brothers on campus.

Connect With Us

Mentorship Programs

Pair with current college brothers to provide guidance, career advice, and professional development support.

Events & Gatherings

Attend BPAA events, anniversary celebrations, and chapter programs that keep the brotherhood strong and connected.

Scholarship Support

Contribute to the Beta Phi Endowed Scholarship at Dillard University — the first BGLO chapter scholarship at the university.

Currently On Campus

College Brothers

Active undergraduate brothers of Beta Phi Chapter at Dillard University — you are the reason BPAA exists. We are here to support you.

Scholarship Support

BPAA's endowed scholarship at Dillard provides financial support to deserving students. College brothers are the direct beneficiaries of decades of alumni giving.

Alumni Network

Access a network of Beta Phi alumni spanning every professional field — from energy and education to IT, healthcare, and civic leadership.

Chapter Support

BPAA provides direct financial, logistical, and mentorship support to the active Beta Phi Chapter — helping brothers succeed both on campus and beyond.
