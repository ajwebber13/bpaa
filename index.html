Who We Are

Built on a legacy of excellence

The Beta Phi Chapter of Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity, Inc. was chartered on November 11, 1938 by eight courageous young men on the campus of Dillard University in New Orleans, Louisiana.

At the 85th anniversary, college and alumni brothers voted to create the Beta Phi Alumni Association — a formal body dedicated to continuing the chapter's legacy of scholarship, service, and brotherhood.

"Manly Deeds, Scholarship, and Love for All Mankind."
